I am James Rafferty Lee

I am James Rafferty Lee. I was born in Surabaya, Indonesia. I am currently a third-year university student in the Tenth of November Institute of Technology. Glad to meet you! I love data and everything related to it.

I was born in Antarctica to a pair of scientists working for the SCP Foundation. From what small information I have eavesdropped, there seemed to be a gigantic creature under the crust of the earth, exactly under the South Pole. It had been dormant for some tens of years, but some records show that its dormancy is tied to a random cycle of some sorts. And through those records, it is speculated that it would be active in a few years. Its activity has been linked to a couple of great disasters in the world, such as the Vladivia Earthquake, the most powerful earthquake ever recorded, and the Aceh Earthquake.

It was a particularly sunny day, for Antarctica of course. I was lounging on a couch in our house (yes, contrary to popular belief, Antarctica does have towns, almost four thousand in population during summertime), when suddenly a beautiful purple cloud localized entirely on our location seemed to materialize out of nowhere. My parents were scrambling as I just stood there watching this unexpectedly breathtaking scenery. My parents shoved me into the bunker, before suddenly taking off. I never saw them ever again. I was given a hefty sum in recompense for my experience and my loss and was given to a loving family here in Indonesia. I have never stopped believing that they were still alive somewhere, out there. Which is why I hope that my skills in computers will someday land me a job in Antarctica, where I can search them. Personally.
